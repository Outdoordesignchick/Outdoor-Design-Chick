Why Plans Matter

The Difference Between
a Dream and a Build

A beautiful 3D rendering gets you excited. A proper set of construction plans gets you built. Contractors can't give you an accurate quote from a rendering alone — and HOA boards won't approve a project without the right documentation.

That's where most outdoor projects stall. Meagen's construction plans package is designed to eliminate both bottlenecks: contractor-ready drawings that let your builder quote confidently, and a complete HOA submission package that puts your application in the best possible position for approval.

"I've been on enough job sites to know what a contractor actually needs from a set of plans. I draw them that way from the start."

Whether you're coming straight from Meagen's 3D design process or arriving with a design from elsewhere, construction plans can be scoped and drawn for your project.

What's Included

Detailed Construction Drawings

Project Types

Construction Plans For

Plans are available for all outdoor living project types — individually or as a combined package when your project spans multiple scopes.

Pool & Spa

Full pool and spa construction drawings including shell shape, bond beam, coping, finish specs, equipment layout, and hydraulic notes.

Landscape

Planting plans with species, quantities, spacing, and placement — plus irrigation zone notes and grade / drainage callouts.

Hardscape

Paving plans showing material types, patterns, dimensions, edge conditions, and transitions between surface materials across the entire outdoor space.

Outdoor Structures

Pergolas, ramadas, shade structures, and outdoor kitchens — plan and elevation drawings with dimensions, materials, and structural callouts for permit and HOA review.

The Process

From Design to
Approved & Ready

Meagen's construction plan process is straightforward by design. You share what you have, she handles the rest — producing drawings that are accurate, complete, and ready to move your project forward.

01

Project Brief

Share your 3D design, concept drawings, or project details. Meagen reviews the scope, confirms what's needed, and provides a custom quote.

02

Site & HOA Review

Property dimensions, setbacks, and HOA submittal requirements are confirmed before drafting begins — so the plans are right the first time.

03

Plan Drafting

Construction drawings are produced to scale with full dimensions, material specifications, and all details your contractor needs to build and bid accurately.

04

HOA Package & Delivery

The complete HOA submission package is assembled and reviewed. Final plan sets are delivered in contractor-ready PDF format, organized and ready to go.

HOA Submissions

Stop Getting Rejected.
Start Getting Approved.

HOA rejections are one of the most common and costly delays in outdoor construction. They almost always come down to the same things: missing documentation, incorrect formatting, or drawings that don't address the association's specific requirements.

Meagen's HOA packages are built around what associations actually want to see — presented clearly, completely, and professionally.

Formatted to Your HOA's Requirements

Every HOA has its own submittal checklist. Meagen reviews yours before preparing the package so nothing is missing and nothing is out of spec.

Site Plan, Elevations & Material Boards

The full visual package associations need to evaluate your project — not just a floor plan, but the complete picture of what the finished space will look like.

Professional Presentation

A well-prepared, clearly labeled submission signals to the HOA review board that this project is serious and well-managed. First impressions matter even in paperwork.

Revision Support if Needed

If the HOA comes back with questions or requests changes, Meagen is available to update the package and keep your approval process moving.
