How It Works

A Thoughtful
Design Process

Every great outdoor space begins with listening. Our process is designed to translate your ideas — however formed — into a precise, buildable vision you'll be proud of for decades.

Get Started
01

Discovery Call

We start with a conversation — your lifestyle, your wishlist, your timeline. No questionnaire, no pressure. Just a real discussion about what your outdoor space can become.

02

Site Assessment

Meagen reviews your property, existing structures, sun patterns, and HOA or city guidelines to establish the full parameters of the design.

03

Concept Design

Initial layouts and design directions are developed, grounding your vision in real proportions and material palettes specific to your home.

04

3D Rendering

Your space comes to life in full photorealistic 3D — day and night views, furniture staging, planting in scale. You'll see exactly what you're building.

05

Construction Documents & Permits

We produce contractor-ready plans, HOA packages, and permit applications — everything needed to break ground without surprises.

06

Build Liaison

Meagen stays engaged through construction — available to your contractor, answering design questions, and ensuring the build stays true to the vision.
