How It Works

From Conversation
to Full 3D Vision

Every rendering starts with listening. Meagen takes the time to understand how you live, what you love, and what your property allows — before a single line is drawn.

Start your project
01

Discovery Call

We talk through your wishlist, your lifestyle, your timeline, and your budget range. No forms, no pressure — just a real conversation about what your outdoor space can become.

02

Site & Property Review

Meagen reviews your property dimensions, existing structures, sun orientation, HOA covenants, and city setbacks — everything that shapes what's possible before the design begins.

03

Concept & Layout

Initial floor plan and design direction developed — pool shape, hardscape zones, structure placement, planting areas, and lighting strategy all mapped out in two dimensions first.

04

3D Build & Rendering

Your space is modeled in full 3D with real materials, textures, plants at scale, and accurate lighting. Multiple camera angles are rendered — aerial, eye-level, detail shots — in both day and night modes.

05

Walkthrough Video

A cinematic video flies through your completed space so you can experience it in motion — not just as a still image. Ideal for sharing with family, contractors, and HOA reviewers.

06

Revisions & Sign-Off

Review your renderings and share feedback. Meagen refines until the design is exactly right — because what gets approved here is what moves forward to construction plans and contractor quoting.

What We Design

Every Element of
Your Outdoor Living Space

From the water to the walls, every feature is designed together — so nothing looks like an afterthought and everything works as one.

Pool & Water Features

Custom pool shapes, spas, water walls, raised bond beams, sun shelves, and water features — designed to anchor the entire space.

Landscape & Planting

Desert-native and resort-style planting plans, scaled to your lot and rendered in full color — grasses, palms, succulents, and shade trees placed with intention.

Hardscape & Paving

Pavers, decking, concrete finishes, retaining walls, steps, and pathways — every surface material selected and visualized in context.

Structures & Shade

Pergolas, ramadas, shade sails, gazebos, and covered outdoor rooms — designed to extend your living space and complement your home's architecture.

Outdoor Kitchen & Living

BBQ islands, bar seating, fire pits, fireplaces, and lounge zones — every entertainment feature positioned and designed for how you actually host.

Lighting Design

Pathway lighting, pool and water illumination, uplighting for trees and structures, and ambient mood lighting — all shown in the night rendering so you see the atmosphere before you buy a fixture.
